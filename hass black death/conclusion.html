BLACK DEATH VS COVID

The Black Death and Covid were both hauge catastophic health crisis which were global and reshaped the society in quite profound ways. The Black Death completely altered medieval Europe and the decline of feudalism and the labour shortage. While Covid 19 was not as deadly as the Black Death it had global repercussions, like accelerating technology adaption, altering having a work life balance and sparking more better care on public health and equality. Both pandemics, while tragic the ultimately spurred huge social and economic transformations, medical advancements and the dynamics of workforce that contributed to a longer term progress/impact.
