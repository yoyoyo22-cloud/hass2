Black Death

The Black Death lasted for approximately 5 years, 1347 to 1352. The Black Death originated in Europe and spread to the Middle East and Asia mainly affecting the countries; India, China, Africa, Persia and Europe. The plague was indiscriminately contagious and very efficient.
